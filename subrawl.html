Subrawl

A 2D retro-mashup game

Roles: Game Designer, C# Scripter

Time: 1 week

Team Size: 2 Designers, 2 2D Artists, 2 Programmers

Tools: Git (GitKraken), Unity, Microsoft Visual Studio

My contribution: Game & UX Design, Ability Iteration, Target seeking AI, some Animations & Animation Implementation

DOWNLOAD FROM ITCH

This project was our one week introduction to Unity. 


The guidelines: Create a retro mashup game. It needed to have at least 3 different retro games incorporated. The game also needed to look and feel retro, but using our own art.


We decided for Lunar Lander, Pac Man, Pong & Bubble Bobble.

About

Subrawl is a local 1v1 game featuring mechanics from retro games such as Bubble Bobble, PacMan, Pong and Lunar Lander.


Break out of bubbles, push your opponent into hazards, and try winning by ANY means nessecary!

Objective & Goals

The main goal of the game was creating a retro mashup game, meaning mixing elements from at least 3 retro games into one cohesive experience. It also needed to have the retro feel and look.


I achieved this by lots of researching and playtesting. We had to find out what the typical nature of a retro game was and replicate it. We decided to go with pick up and play - master through repetition, as well as a super clear UX, piggybacking on the retro game UI standards. 


The objective of the game is being the last one standing. Avoid the dangers and kill your opponent by pushing them into environmental hazards or activating pick ups.
