About me


I’m a Level & UX Designer based in Stockholm, currently studying at Futuregames.


I focus on building inspiring experiences with smooth flows, as well as accessibility and readability.

I enjoy challenging myself with scripting, rapid prototyping and using level design or dressing as a tool to convey emotions or tell a story.


On my free time I like to play others games, or create my own. I mostly play story driven games, MMORPG, sand box or puzzle games, but I enjoy all categories.


I've also got a big interest in nature and plants, and I have a chilli plant collection.


Feel free to contact me if you have any questions!
